# Foundational Strength Builder

**Program ID:** 15
**Duration:** 2 weeks
**Created:** 2025-10-25 01:13

## Description

This 2-week program is designed for intermediate lifters aiming to build foundational strength through barbell training. It incorporates key compound movements with progressive overload to maximize strength gains while ensuring muscle balance across all major muscle groups. Ideal for those looking to establish a solid base in their lifting journey.
